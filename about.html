GILK was established in 1996 and since then we have become a multipurpose leasing company that has successfully executed more than 180 deals of direct financial leasing and leaseback. The structure of our leasing portfolio is diversified and allowed us to attract over 15 billion rubles of loans from various banks and investors.

Our advantages
Why choose us It is
General
Convenient
We take full responsibility for obtaining financing: we communicate with creditors, build financial models and prepare all the papers.
Fast
We work quickly, even in the most complicated cases deals are executed within 2-3 months from the date the application is filled.
Safe
We have a perfect credit history; we have proven ourselves as a reliable partner on the financial services market.
Practical
We provide an individual leasing service that meets the client`s needs, we help to structure deals in the way that is the most efficient for the client.
Financial
Inexpencive
Compared with the loan, the lessee receives saves significantly on income and property tax, as the leasing company can attract money at lower rates.
Rational
Flexible payment system: it is possible to develop various leasing payment schedules that take into account the needs of the lessee. The client can accurately calculate the budget of the project for the duration of the leasing agreement and easily manage cash flows.
Efficient
Leasing gives the lessee the possibility to use the necessary means of production without a one-time payment of their full cost.
Optimal
Accelerated depreciation of the leasing object: the possibility to promptly update out-of-date equipment, reduction of taxable profit
